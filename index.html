The Founder

Where Science
Meets Tradition

Abhish Ayurveda was founded with a singular conviction: that the ancient science of Ayurveda, when guided by rigorous clinical training and evidence-based thinking, can transform modern health.

The practice is led by a BAMS-qualified Ayurvedic physician with an MBA in Healthcare Management and a PhD from the Faculty of Management Studies (FMS), University of Delhi — bringing together deep classical knowledge with the strategic lens of contemporary healthcare.
